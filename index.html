What is Zafaria 2k24?

"Zafaria 2k24" is the name of this year's Rabee' festival, organized by the Darunnajath Islamic Complex and Iqra' Mates. In a rapidly evolving contemporary context rife with falsehoods and fake news, Zafaria 2k24 aims to uncover and present the truth and facts about Mugals. Zafaria 2k24 Arts Fest will play a pivotal role in enhancing the academic progress of students across various fields of study and academic activities.

Zafar which means “victory” in Arabic and Persian. This name carries historical significance, particularly through Bahadur Shah Zafar, the last Mughal emperor, who symbolizes the end of an era but also reflects the grandeur and cultural depth of the Mughal dynasty.
